Course information

Courses also comply with the Health & Safety Executives Guidance HSG6 & Approved Code of Practice L117 and in following both the guidance and code of practice you can be assured that the training is sufficient to comply with the law.

Basic/Novice

A Basic course is designed to take a complete beginner and turn him/her into a confident and competent operator whether it be on a basic FLT or a larger more complicated piece of equipment. Candidates will be acquainted with HASAWA, LOLER and PUWER

Experienced & Semi - skilled operators

A Semi-skilled course is designed for those candidates that have experience but may never have had the benefit of formal training or testing. Candidates will be acquainted with the HSAW act LOLAR and PUWER

Re test Refresher

This course is designed to re-affirm skills that the candidate has acquired with experience, it also reacquaints candidates with the HASAWA, LOLER and PUWER legislation as it applies to them while operating industrial equipment. HSG 36 Guidelines state that certificates should be renewed every 3/5 years so this course is ideal for candidates that have had a certificate that is over 3/5 years old and wish to keep within the HSE Guide lines.

Conversion

This course is designed for the experienced operator who wishes to add to their skills by learning to handle new equipment.

Familiarisation

Familiarisation courses are designed for those candidates who already have a certificate but they may wish to add attachments to their skills.

All training will be given by highly motivated and highly qualified instructors with many years experience on the premises
